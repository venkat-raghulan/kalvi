Unlike the traditional programs, Kalvi's curriculum is built based on present-day benchmarks and future requirements to equip students with all the skills required to solve complex global challenges. Apart from domain skills, the program also natively focuses on developing the personal and interpersonal skills of the students.

Students get to work with partner companies from Semester 3 onwards with an average earning potential of up to INR 8.2 Lakhs. Upon graduation, they are likely to get 3x the entry salary in comparison with regular grads.

The program is delivered in blended mode powered by Quadrangle - A smart delivery platform. It follows a flipped classroom model where class-time is reserved for problem-solving and application.

Upon completing the program, students would receive a recognised degree from the partner university accredited by the regulatory body. It will either be a B.tech/B.Sc degree depending on the campus chosen at the time of admission.
